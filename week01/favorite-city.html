My Favorite City

My favorite city so far is actually my hometown, in Sandpoint. I find this little city up in north idaho has it all. From a large warm lake, to mountain hikes, and a skii resort less than 10 minutes away from my house. There is always something to do in Sandpoint, and if you love the outdoors, which I do, You'll never get bored of the scenic views of the city wherever you go. It's beautiful and a great place to live.
