Kootenai Falls Whitewater Rafting

Welcome to Kootenai Falls White Water Rafting! Here we believe in providing a safe environment for all members of the family to experience the rush of adrenaline that comes with this once in a life time opportunity to ride nature's most exhillerating roller coaster!

History

Here at Kootenai Falls White Water Rafting, we began as a group of friends looking for an adventure. We loved going for rapid rides throughout the country, and we like many, wanted to have a white water rafting experience near our hometown! We started with our small group of friends, and that small group of friends grew month after month, year after year, till the Kootenai White Water Rafting Company was created to provide a safe, easy way for everyone to experience the Joy that we felt while white water rafting
